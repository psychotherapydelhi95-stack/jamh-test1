Author Guidelines

1. Manuscript Categories

  • Original Articles (2500–4500 words)
  • Review Articles (3000–6000 words)
  • Case Studies (1500–2500 words)
  • Short Communications (1000–1500 words)

2. Manuscript Formatting

  • Font: Times New Roman, 12 pt
  • Spacing: 1.5 line spacing
  • Margins: 1 inch on all sides
  • Submit manuscript in PDF or DOCX format
  • All pages should be numbered

3. Structure of Manuscript

  • Title Page (Title, Authors, Affiliations, Email, Corresponding Author)
  • Abstract (150–250 words)
  • Keywords (3–6 keywords)
  • Introduction
  • Methodology
  • Results
  • Discussion
  • Conclusion
  • References (APA style)

4. Reference Style (APA 7th Edition)

  • Follow APA 7th Edition formatting.
  • Examples:
    • Journal Article: Author, A. A. (Year). Title of article. Journal Name, volume(issue), pages.
    • Book: Author, A. A. (Year). Book title. Publisher.

5. Plagiarism Policy

  • Acceptable similarity index: below 10%
  • Higher similarity will be returned for revision

6. Ethics & Approval

  • Research involving human subjects requires Ethics Committee approval
  • Upload the ethics letter in PDF format
  • Include a Conflict of Interest statement

7. Cover Letter

Attach a cover letter including:

  • Statement of originality
  • Confirmation that the article is not under review elsewhere
  • Names of all authors
